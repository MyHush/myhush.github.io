Hush Privacy Policy


Hush, at it's core is a peer-to-peer network and there is no central location where metadata is stored. Our policy is to store as little metadata as possible, or not at all. Hush is not a company or foundation, we are a decentralized cypherpunk community and continue to research the bleeding edge in privacy.

When using some Hush software, there are mandatory or optional features that require talking to third party websites and APIs. We cannot control how they use metadata, such as IP address and browser details.


  • Cloudflare - Denial-of-Service protection and SSL

  • Coingecko - price feeds

  • Linode - server hosting

A non-exhaustive list of service providers that Hush uses are: If you do not want your metadata to potentially go to these places, we highly recommend using Tor and/or the TAILS, Whonix or Qubes operating systems with Hush, which integrate Tor.
